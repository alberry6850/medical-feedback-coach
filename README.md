# Medical Feedback Coach

An interactive web application designed to help medical school faculty practice delivering effective feedback to struggling students in a supportive, guided environment.

## Features

### 🎯 Interactive Feedback Practice
- **Realistic Scenarios**: Faculty encounter a medical student with multiple performance and professionalism deficiencies
- **Multi-Round Practice**: Three rounds of feedback practice with progressive scenarios
- **Student Simulation**: AI-powered student responses that react to the quality of feedback provided

### 🤖 AI Coach Guidance
- **Real-Time Tips**: Get coaching tips before giving feedback
- **Analysis & Feedback**: Receive detailed analysis of each feedback attempt including:
  - Strengths in feedback delivery
  - Opportunities for improvement
  - Best practices guidance
- **Evidence-Based Approach**: Coaching grounded in educational psychology and faculty development research

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- **Student Progress Visualization**: See how the student progresses (or struggles) in five key areas:
  - Professionalism & Dress Code
  - Punctuality & Presence
  - Engagement & Receptiveness
  - Documentation Quality
  - Team Collaboration
- **Realistic Improvement**: Student progress varies based on feedback quality

### 💡 Learning Resources
- **Best Practices Guide**: Access evidence-based feedback techniques including:
  - Being Specific: Use concrete examples
  - Listen First: Ask open-ended questions
  - Use I-Statements: Frame feedback non-accusatorily
  - Balance with Strengths: Maintain psychological safety
  - Focus on Growth: Frame challenges as development opportunities
  - Collaborate on Planning: Co-create solutions

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
2. Click "Meet Your Student" to view the student profile
3. Click "Begin Feedback Practice" to start
4. Type your feedback in the textarea
5. Use the "Get Coach Tip" button for guidance if needed
6. Click "Give Feedback" to submit
7. Review the coach analysis and student response
8. Progress through three rounds

## How It Works

### Feedback Evaluation
The AI coach analyzes your feedback for:
- **Specificity**: Concrete examples vs. generalizations
- **Non-accusatory tone**: I-statements vs. judgmental language
- **Support orientation**: Collaborative vs. punitive approach
- **Balance**: Recognition of strengths alongside challenges
- **Reflection prompts**: Open-ended questions

### Student Response Generation
The simulated student's response depends on feedback quality:
- **High-quality feedback** (specific, supportive, I-statements): Positive, receptive response
- **Mixed feedback**: Mixed response with some defensiveness
- **Low-quality feedback**: Defensive reaction

### Progress Calculation
Student progress increases based on:
- Whether feedback addressed relevant areas
- Quality of feedback delivery (best practices adherence)
- Consistency across rounds

## Educational Foundation

This application incorporates principles from:
- **Feedback Theory**: Hattie & Timperley (2007) on effective feedback
- **Difficult Conversations**: Stone, Patton, & Heen framework
- **Psychological Safety**: Edmondson's research on learning environments
- **Adult Learning**: Knowles' principles of andragogy
- **Clinical Education**: Best practices from medical education research
